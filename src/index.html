About me

Hello, I'm Kristina Maksimovic. Just recently, I successfully completed my Mittlere Reife and now I'm thrilled to kickstart my career as an aspiring photographer and videographer. Creativity runs through my veins, and I have an innate ability to spot even the tiniest details. My passion lies in visually portraying people, capturing their essence through the lens of my camera. I pride myself on my sharp observational skills, which allow me to notice the subtleties that others might miss. I'm excited to dive into the world of photography and videography, where I can turn my creativity and keen eye for detail into captivating visual stories. This journey ahead promises to be an exhilarating one, and I'm eager to see where it takes me as I hone my skills and pursue my passion.

In my spare time, I'm an avid photographer and film enthusiast. I love capturing moments behind the lens and analyzing films for their cinematography and storytelling. Traveling is also a big passion of mine, having explored six countries already. Additionally, I enjoy delving into video and photo editing with software like Photoshop and Adobe After Effects, refining my artistic creations. My ultimate goal is to turn my passion into my profession and become a professional videographer and photographer.
